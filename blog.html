Deep Learning:

Deep learning is the area of artificial learning that focuses on building neural networks. The topics are vast but mainly include the following:

  • computer vision: teaching the computer to train on images/video and "learn" patterns and analyze untrained images (e.g. self-driving cars recognizing if the object infront of the car is a person or a stop sign)
  • Natural Language Processing: teaching the computer to learn patterns of Language and speach recognition (e.g. speech recognition in alexa/google home devices)
  • Recommender Systems: teaching the computer to recognize patterns in tabular data and provide recommendations based on this data (e.g. What to watch next on Netflix)
